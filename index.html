Escape From North Korea

The Story, Art, Graphics, and Programming are by Adam Robertson. W A S D to move, and A to move on the bike. Do not choose 'leave for work' until you have explored the neighborhood and your house. After leaving for work, make sure to press space to get off the bike and explore each room to find all the signs which contain information. If buildings appear cut off, walk around them to see the whole thing. At the beginning, the player has no incentive to leave. But then...
